O Canada! Our home and native land! True patriot love in all thy sons command. With glowing hearts we see thee rise, The True North strong and free! Land of our forefathers your front is wreathed with a glorious garland of flowers. This song is about the nation of Canada. The English version is about how the people of this nation will stand on guard to protect it. The French version of the song has different lyrics and is about Canada's glory and how it will protect us.
